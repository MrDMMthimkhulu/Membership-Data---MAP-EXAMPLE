Inspiring Others Towards Rural Health

The Rural Doctors Association of Southern Africa exists because rural healthcare is not a simplified version of urban healthcare. It is its own discipline, shaped by geography, scarcity, resilience, and deep community connection.

Who We Are

RuDASA was founded in 1996 and represents healthcare professionals working across South Africa’s rural landscape.

Vision and Mission

Vision: Quality healthcare for all rural people.

Mission: Support and empower rural healthcare workers.

What We Do

RuDASA was founded in 1996 by a group of committed rural doctors who recognized that rural healthcare professionals face unique professional, clinical, and systemic challenges. What began as a regional initiative has grown into a national network representing doctors and allied health professionals working across South Africa’s rural landscape. The organisation functions as a professional home for those practicing in rural and remote settings, offering connection in environments where professional isolation is often the norm.
